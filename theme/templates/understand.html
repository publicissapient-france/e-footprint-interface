How do we model the CO2 footprint of a digital service?

What are the different components of a digital service that come into e-footprint’s calculation of its carbon footprint?

Hardware

A digital service’s carbon footprint comes from the fabrication of the hardware required to run and use the service, and from the electricity required to power that hardware.

Usage
